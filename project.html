My Projects

FurEver

This project aims to develop a comprehensive online platform to streamline the pet adoption process, bridging the gap between potential adopters and shelter animals The platform provides a user-friendly interface where individuals can easily browse profiles of dogs and cats available for adoption The adoption process is facilitated through clear guidelines, including application submission and adoption contract signing. Additionally, educational information on responsible pet ownership and care are provided to support new pet parents This platform seeks to increase the number of shelter animals finding loving homes while promoting responsible pet ownership within the community.

Sahayak

A web portal designed to assist senior citizens with various services such as healthcare information, appointment scheduling, community events, online consultations, and access to resources. The platform can also provide features for caregivers and family members to manage and monitor senior citizens' needs.
